# About Originfacts

## Smarter travel research, all in one place

Originfacts is a travel information and comparison website created to help travellers find useful travel resources, compare prices, and discover options for flights, hotels, car rentals, and related travel services.

Our goal is simple: to make travel research easier by bringing information from many travel providers into one place, so visitors can compare options, review useful details, and make more informed travel decisions.

Originfacts is available to visitors around the world and is operated by **FXN HOLDINGS LIMITED**.

## What we do

Originfacts helps users explore travel options such as:

- cheap flights;
- hotels and accommodation;
- car rentals;
- travel tips;
- destination information;
- travel resources;
- comparison tables;
- live or estimated travel prices;
- user reviews, ratings, comments, photos, forum posts, and travel tips.

We aim to give visitors useful information before they click through to a third-party provider to complete a booking or purchase.

## How Originfacts works

Originfacts is an affiliate travel aggregation website. This means we may display travel information, links, widgets, offers, rankings, and comparison content from third-party providers and affiliate partners.

When you click a link on Originfacts, you may be taken to a third-party travel website, such as a booking platform, airline, hotel provider, car rental provider, tour provider, or other travel service.

We do not directly sell travel bookings, take payments, issue tickets, operate hotels, operate airlines, rent vehicles, provide tours, process refunds, or manage cancellations.

Your booking or purchase is made directly with the third-party provider you choose.

## Affiliate disclosure

Originfacts may earn an affiliate commission when visitors click links on our website and later make a booking or purchase through a third-party provider.

This usually does not increase the price you pay.

We currently use Travelpayouts as an affiliate network. Affiliate relationships may influence which travel links, offers, widgets, or providers appear on the website.

We aim to keep affiliate and commercial relationships clear and transparent for our users.

## Prices, availability, and rankings

Travel prices and availability can change quickly.

The prices shown on Originfacts may be live, estimated, cached, promotional, converted from another currency, or based on provider data available at the time.

Before booking, always check the final price, taxes, fees, cancellation rules, refund terms, baggage rules, local charges, deposit requirements, and other conditions on the third-party provider's website.

Where we use words such as "cheap", "cheapest", "best", "popular", or "recommended", these are based on available data, user search criteria, provider information, affiliate feeds, editorial judgment, or commercial relationships where applicable.

Originfacts may not compare every provider or every available travel option in the market.
